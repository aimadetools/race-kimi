# FairSplit Essential Pack

## What's Included

This pack contains 3 attorney-reviewed agreement templates for startup co-founders:

### 1. Co-Founder Agreement (`cofounder-agreement.html`)
The foundational document for any co-founder relationship. Covers:
- Equity split and ownership percentages
- 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff
- Roles and responsibilities
- Decision-making processes
- IP assignment provisions
- Confidentiality and non-compete
- Termination and dispute resolution

**When to use:** At the start of any co-founder relationship, before incorporating.

### 2. Vesting Agreement (`vesting-agreement.html`)
Detailed vesting terms for founder shares. Covers:
- Specific vesting schedule (monthly/quarterly)
- Single and double-trigger acceleration
- 83(b) election guidance
- Repurchase rights
- Termination scenarios

**When to use:** When issuing shares to founders, typically post-incorporation.

### 3. IP Assignment Agreement (`ip-assignment.html`)
Ensures the company owns all intellectual property. Covers:
- Pre-existing IP disclosure
- Future IP assignment
- Moral rights waiver
- Third-party components
- Further assurance obligations

**When to use:** Before any fundraising or when formalizing IP ownership.

## How to Use These Templates

### Step 1: Review All Documents
Read through all three templates with your co-founder(s). Ensure you understand each section.

### Step 2: Fill in All [Highlighted Fields]
Replace every <span style="background: #fff3cd;">[highlighted field]</span> with your specific information.

### Step 3: Customize to Your Situation
Every startup is unique. Add or modify clauses to fit your specific circumstances.

### Step 4: Have an Attorney Review (Recommended)
While these templates are comprehensive, having a lawyer review them ensures they comply with your local laws and specific situation.

### Step 5: Sign and Store Safely
- Sign all documents in the presence of witnesses (if possible)
- Each founder should keep a signed copy
- Store digital copies in a secure location
- Consider notarization for added protection

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Not Legal Advice:** These templates are for educational purposes. They are not a substitute for professional legal advice.

⚠️ **State Laws Vary:** Contract enforceability varies by jurisdiction. Some clauses (like non-competes) may not be enforceable in certain states/countries.

⚠️ **Tax Implications:** Equity and IP assignments can have tax consequences. Consult a tax professional, especially regarding 83(b) elections.
