4. VESTING SCHEDULE

All shares issued to Founders shall be subject to a [4]-year vesting schedule with a [1]-year cliff.

Vesting Terms:

  • Cliff Period: No shares vest during the first [12] months
  • After Cliff: [25]% of shares vest immediately upon completion of the cliff period
  • Ongoing Vesting: Remaining shares vest monthly/quarterly over the remaining [36] months
  • Acceleration: 100% acceleration upon a Change of Control (acquisition)

Leaving Before Cliff: If a Founder leaves before the cliff period ends, they forfeit all unvested shares and receive no equity.

Leaving After Cliff: If a Founder leaves after the cliff period, they retain vested shares and forfeit unvested shares.

10. NON-COMPETE

During their involvement with the Company and for a period of [12/24] months thereafter, Founders agree not to engage in any business that directly competes with the Company's core business.

Note: Non-compete enforceability varies by jurisdiction. Consult an attorney to ensure compliance with local laws.

11. TERMINATION

A Founder's involvement may be terminated under the following circumstances:

  • Voluntary Resignation: [30/60] days written notice required
  • For Cause: Material breach of this Agreement, gross negligence, fraud, or criminal conduct
  • Without Cause: By unanimous decision of other Founders

Upon termination, unvested shares are forfeited. The Company or remaining Founders have the right to purchase vested shares at fair market value.

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall first be addressed through good-faith negotiation between Founders.
