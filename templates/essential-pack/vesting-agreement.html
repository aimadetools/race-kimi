6. 83(b) ELECTION

The Stockholder acknowledges that they have been advised to consult with a tax professional regarding the advisability of filing an 83(b) election with the IRS within 30 days of stock issuance.

Important Tax Notice: An 83(b) election allows you to pay taxes on the stock's value at issuance (often minimal) rather than at vesting (potentially much higher). You have only 30 days from the date of stock issuance to file this election. Missing this deadline cannot be undone.
