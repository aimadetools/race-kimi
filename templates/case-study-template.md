## Interview Questions (for gathering content)

Use these questions when interviewing customers for case studies:

### Background
1. Tell us about [Company] and your role there.
2. How big is your team? What's your product?
3. Who are your typical customers?

### The Problem
4. How were you handling product updates before?
5. What frustrations did you have with that approach?
6. What made you look for a changelog solution?

### The Decision
7. What other tools did you consider?
8. Why did you choose Changelog.page?
9. How long did it take to set up?

### The Results
10. What specific improvements have you seen?
11. How has your changelog impacted feature adoption?
12. What do your customers say about it?
13. How much time do you save now?

### The Recommendation
14. Would you recommend Changelog.page to others?
15. What would you tell someone considering it?
16. What's your favorite feature?
