Why SaaS Products Need a Changelog

Invisible updates are wasted updates. If users don't know about features, they can't adopt them. If they don't adopt them, they churn.

🎯

Drive Feature Adoption

Announce new features where users will actually see them. Use screenshots, GIFs, and clear explanations to show value, not just list changes.

💰

Reduce Churn

Users churn when they don't see value. A changelog demonstrates continuous improvement and helps customers justify their subscription month after month.
