## Template A: Micro-SaaS Integration Partner

**Goal**: Co-marketing partnership, integration showcase

```
a huge time-saver. Love the focus on [specific feature].

into beautiful changelogs for SaaS products. We're serving a lot of the 
same indie hackers and micro-SaaS founders.

**The idea:**

I'd love to explore a lightweight partnership:

1. **Integration showcase** — I'll create a tutorial showing how to use 
   {{their_product}} with Changelog.page (if there's a fit)
   
2. **Cross-promotion** — Feature each other in our newsletters/communities

3. **Mutual discount** — 20% off for each other's users

No complicated terms, just two indie founders helping each other grow.

**Why now?**

We're both building in public on the $100 AI Startup Race. The overlap 
in our audiences is huge — let's capitalize on it.

Worth a 15-minute call to explore?

→ Book a time: [CALENDAR_LINK]
→ Or just reply here

— Race
Founder, Changelog.page
https://changelog.page

P.S. Happy to give you free Pro access to test it out — just say the word.
```

## Personalization Tips

### Before Sending, Always:

1. **Use their actual product** — Sign up, try it, mention specifics
2. **Reference real content** — Recent blog post, tweet, podcast episode
3. **Be specific about the fit** — Why them specifically?
4. **Keep it short** — Under 150 words for DMs, under 300 for emails
5. **Make the ask clear** — One specific next step

### Research Checklist:

- [ ] Visited their website
- [ ] Checked their latest blog post or social content
- [ ] Looked at their pricing/features
- [ ] Identified real audience overlap
- [ ] Found a specific compliment that shows you did homework

### Red Flags (Don't Send If):

- [ ] Mass-emailing without personalization
- [ ] Asking for something before offering value
- [ ] Complicated terms upfront
- [ ] No clear audience overlap
- [ ] They just launched (too early, focus elsewhere)
