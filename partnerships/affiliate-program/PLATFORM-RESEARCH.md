# Affiliate Platform Research — Changelog.page

Comparison of affiliate platforms for running the Changelog.page affiliate program.

---

## Platform Comparison

### Option 1: Rewardful (Recommended)

**URL**: https://rewardful.com

| Criteria | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Pricing** | $29/mo (up to $5K MRR), $79/mo (up to $50K MRR) |
| **Commission** | Unlimited affiliates, unlimited referrals |
| **Cookie Duration** | Custom (default 60 days) |
| **Payout** | PayPal, Stripe Connect |
| **Integration** | JavaScript snippet + webhooks |
| **Stripe Integration** | Native — auto-tracks Stripe subscriptions |

**Pros:**
- Built for SaaS (Stripe integration)
- Simple setup (15 minutes)
- Beautiful affiliate dashboard
- Automatic recurring commission tracking
- No transaction fees on top of monthly fee

**Cons:**
- Monthly cost (not free)
- Requires Stripe for best experience

**Best For:** SaaS companies using Stripe

---

### Option 2: Tapfiliate

**URL**: https://tapfiliate.com

| Criteria | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Pricing** | $59/mo (Essential), $149/mo (Pro) |
| **Commission** | Unlimited affiliates |
| **Cookie Duration** | Custom |
| **Payout** | PayPal, bank transfer |
| **Integration** | Multiple options (JS, API, plugins) |
| **Features** | Multi-level marketing, bonus campaigns |

**Pros:**
- More mature platform
- Multi-level marketing support
- Extensive integrations
- White-label options

**Cons:**
- More expensive
- Overkill for simple programs
- Steeper learning curve

**Best For:** Larger programs, multi-tier affiliates

---

### Option 3: FirstPromoter

**URL**: https://firstpromoter.com

| Criteria | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Pricing** | $49/mo (Growth), $149/mo (Scale) |
| **Commission** | Unlimited affiliates |
| **Cookie Duration** | Custom |
| **Payout** | PayPal, Wise, bank transfer |
| **Integration** | Stripe, Paddle, Chargebee |

**Pros:**
- Built specifically for SaaS
- Great affiliate onboarding
- Referral tracking for customers too
- Widgets for in-app promotion

**Cons:**
- Mid-range pricing
- Limited customization

**Best For:** SaaS wanting customer + affiliate referrals

---

### Option 4: LemonSqueezy (All-in-One)

**URL**: https://lemonsqueezy.com

| Criteria | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Pricing** | 5% + 50¢ per transaction (includes affiliate) |
| **Commission** | Built-in affiliate system |
| **Cookie Duration** | 30 days |
| **Payout** | Handles all payouts automatically |
| **Integration** | Replace entire payment stack |

**Pros:**
- Affiliate program included
- No monthly fee (pay per transaction)
- Handles taxes (VAT, sales tax)
- Merchant of record (less liability)

**Cons:**
- Requires switching payment processor
- Higher transaction fees
- Less control over affiliate program

**Best For:** New products wanting simple setup

---

### Option 5: Self-Hosted (Simple)

**DIY with:**
- Database for affiliate tracking
- Unique referral codes
- Manual commission calculation
- PayPal Mass Pay for payouts

**Pros:**
- Free (minus payment processing)
- Full control
- No platform dependency

**Cons:**
- Development time required
- Manual payout management
- No affiliate dashboard
- Risk of errors

**Best For:** Technical teams with limited budget

---

## Recommendation

### For Changelog.page: **Rewardful**

**Rationale:**
1. **Stripe Integration** — When we add Stripe billing, Rewardful auto-tracks everything
2. **Affordable** — $29/mo at our current scale
3. **SaaS-Focused** — Built for recurring revenue businesses
4. **Quick Setup** — Can launch affiliate program in 1 day
5. **Recurring Commissions** — Automatically tracks and pays 30% recurring

### Implementation Timeline

| 1 | Week 6-8 | Set up Stripe billing |
| 2 | Week 8 | Add Rewardful integration |

## Commission Structure

### Proposed Terms

| Tier | Commission | Cookie | Requirements |
|------|------------|--------|--------------|
| Standard | 30% recurring | 60 days | Anyone can join |
| Partner | 35% recurring | 90 days | 10+ paid referrals |
| VIP | 40% recurring | 120 days | 50+ paid referrals |

### Payment Terms

- **Minimum payout**: $50
- **Payout schedule**: Monthly (15th of month)
- **Method**: PayPal or bank transfer
- **Hold period**: 30 days (refund protection)
