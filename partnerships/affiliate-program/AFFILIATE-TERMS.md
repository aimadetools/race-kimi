## Program Overview

The Changelog.page Affiliate Program allows you to earn commissions by referring customers to Changelog.page. When someone signs up using your unique referral link, you earn a percentage of their subscription fees.

### Standard Tier

- **Commission Rate**: 30% recurring
- **Cookie Duration**: 60 days
- **Eligible Products**: All paid plans (Pro $15/mo, Team $49/mo)
- **Commission Period**: Lifetime of the customer subscription

### Partner Tier (10+ paid referrals)

- **Commission Rate**: 35% recurring
- **Cookie Duration**: 90 days
- **Additional Benefits**: Priority support, early access to features

### VIP Tier (50+ paid referrals)

- **Commission Rate**: 40% recurring
- **Cookie Duration**: 120 days
- **Additional Benefits**: Custom partnership terms, co-marketing opportunities

| Pro | $15 | $4.50 | $54 |

**Example**: Refer 10 Pro customers = $540/year passive income

## Tracking & Attribution

### How It Works
1. Share your unique referral link
2. User clicks link (cookie set for 60 days)
3. User signs up within 60 days
4. Commission attributed to you

### Cookie Details
- **Duration**: 60 days (Standard tier)
- **Last-click attribution**: Most recent affiliate gets credit
- **Device tracking**: Cross-device tracking supported

### What Counts as a Referral
- User signs up using your link
- User upgrades to paid plan within cookie window
- User maintains active subscription

### What Does NOT Count
- Self-referrals (you cannot refer yourself)
- Existing customers (must be new signup)
- Refunded/cancelled subscriptions (clawback applies)
- Fraudulent signups
