## Commission Details

### Recurring Explained

You earn 30% of every payment the customer makes — not just the first one.

**Example:**
- Customer signs up for Pro Monthly via your link
- Month 1: You earn $3.60
- Month 2: You earn $3.60
- Month 3: You earn $3.60
- ...and so on until they cancel

### Cancellation Policy

If a customer cancels:
- You stop earning commissions from that customer
- Previous earned commissions are not clawed back
- If they reactivate within 90 days, commissions resume

### Refund Policy

If a customer requests a refund:
- Commission for that payment is voided
- If already paid out, amount deducted from next payout

### Attribution

- Last-click attribution (60-day cookie)
- If user clicks multiple affiliate links, last one wins
- Direct traffic after cookie expires = no commission
