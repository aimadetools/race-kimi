## Posting Guidelines

**Best times to post on Indie Hackers:**
- Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM EST
- Avoid Mondays (buried in weekend posts) and Fridays (low engagement)

**Formatting tips:**
- Use emoji bullets for readability
- Include specific numbers (be transparent)
- Ask questions to drive engagement
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours

**Cross-promotion:**
- Share IH posts to Twitter/LinkedIn
- Include IH link in newsletter
- Reference IH discussions in future posts
