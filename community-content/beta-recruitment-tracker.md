## Beta User Criteria

### Ideal Candidate Profile
- [ ] Has an active SaaS product or open source project
- [ ] Currently shipping updates (at least monthly)
- [ ] Doesn't have a changelog OR unhappy with current solution
- [ ] Willing to provide feedback within 1 week
- [ ] Comfortable with 15-min video call

### Auto-Accept Signals
- Active product with users
- Clear use case for changelog
- Previous engagement with product (waitlist, social)
- Technical background (can self-serve)

### Auto-Decline Signals
- No active product (idea stage only)
- No time for feedback
- Just wants free Pro (no intention to use)
- Outside target market (e.g., non-tech business)

- 1 = Just wants free stuff
