Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, you'll keep your Pro features until the end of your billing period.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. The Free plan requires no credit card. You can use Changelog.page indefinitely for free. We only ask for payment information when you choose to upgrade.

What happens if I cancel?

Your changelog remains accessible, but Pro features will be disabled after your billing period ends. You can reactivate at any time and your settings will be restored.

Is there a limit on visitors?

No. All plans include unlimited visitors and bandwidth. Your changelog is served via a global CDN, so it will remain fast no matter how popular it gets.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with Changelog.page for any reason, contact us within 14 days for a full refund.

Can I get a custom plan?

For teams with specific needs (more seats, SLA, custom integrations), contact us and we'll create a custom plan that works for you.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
