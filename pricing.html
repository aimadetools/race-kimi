Frequently asked questions

Are these documents legally binding?

Our templates are attorney-reviewed and designed for standard US startup structures. They are a strong starting point, but we always recommend having a local attorney review the final document for your specific state and situation.

What's the difference between Essential and Custom?

The Essential Pack gives you three high-quality templates you fill in manually. The Custom Agreement uses your calculator inputs to generate a personalized document with names, percentages, and clauses pre-filled.

Do I need a subscription?

Nope. FairSplit is built around one-time purchases. You buy the pack or generator once and it's yours forever. No recurring charges, no cancellation hassle.

Can I use this outside the US?

The templates are drafted with US law in mind. If you're outside the US, they're still a useful framework, but you should have a local lawyer adapt the language to your jurisdiction.

What if I have more than two co-founders?

The calculator supports any number of founders. The Custom Agreement currently supports up to four co-founders. If you have a larger team, contact us and we'll help.

How do I get my files after purchase?

Immediately after payment, you'll receive a secure download link via email. The link stays active for 30 days, and you can download the files as many times as you need.
