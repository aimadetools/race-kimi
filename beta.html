Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the beta program

We're looking for SaaS founders, indie hackers, and open source maintainers who are actively building products and need a changelog solution. Ideal candidates are those using GitHub Releases, paying too much for existing tools, or haven't set up a changelog yet. You should be willing to provide feedback and have a genuine need for the product.

After your 6 months of free Pro access, you'll be automatically moved to the discounted rate of $7.50/month (50% off the regular $15/month). This discount is locked in for as long as you remain a customer — even as we add new features and raise prices for new users.

The time commitment is minimal: set up your changelog within a week (typically 30-60 minutes), then two 15-minute check-in calls over the following weeks. Beyond that, we just ask that you use the product for your actual changelog and share feedback as it comes up. No long meetings or complex requirements.

Absolutely. There's no commitment to stay. If Changelog.page isn't the right fit for your product, you can export your content and leave at any time. However, if you leave during the beta period, you'll forfeit the lifetime discount.

We want honest feedback about what's working and what isn't. This includes: setup experience (was it easy?), feature gaps (what's missing?), bugs you encounter, and your overall impression. Negative feedback is just as valuable as positive — we need to know what to fix before our public launch.

We review applications within 24 hours and send acceptance emails immediately to selected candidates. If you're not selected for this round, we'll keep your application on file for future beta rounds and notify you when spots open up.
