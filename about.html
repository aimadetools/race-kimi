Our Story

Every SaaS founder knows the problem: you've built something great, shipped new features, fixed bugs, improved performance — but your users don't know about it.

GitHub Releases are developer-friendly but customer-hostile. Statuspage.io and Beamer are powerful but expensive, starting at $29-50/month for features most startups don't need. Custom solutions require maintenance you can't afford.

We saw a gap: a changelog tool that's beautiful, affordable, and dead simple. No widgets slowing down your site. No complex setup. Just write Markdown, get a stunning changelog.

So we built Changelog.page — the changelog tool we wish existed when we were launching our own products.

What we believe

Your changelog should load instantly, anywhere in the world. Static sites on a global CDN make this possible.

Affordable pricing

Startups shouldn't pay enterprise prices. Our Pro plan is half the cost of competitors with no feature bloat.

Own your data
