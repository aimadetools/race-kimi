About FairSplit

We believe starting a company should be exciting—not a negotiation battlefield. FairSplit exists to give co-founders a fair, transparent way to agree on equity.

The problem we solve

Most co-founders split equity one of two ways: they default to 50/50 because it feels safe, or they hire a lawyer and burn through thousands of dollars before writing a single line of code. Both approaches are broken.

50/50 splits ignore real differences in time, money, skills, and IP. And lawyer fees are a brutal tax on pre-revenue startups. Worse, unclear equity agreements are a top reason co-founder relationships explode—often killing the company.

FairSplit replaces guessing with a transparent, numbers-driven conversation. Our free calculator helps you quantify each founder's contribution. Our agreement templates and generator make it official—fast, affordable, and fair.

How we’re different

We're not a law firm, and we don't pretend to be. We're builders who think legal clarity should be as accessible as a landing page builder.

Here's what sets FairSplit apart:

  • Transparent math. You see exactly how the calculator arrives at every percentage. No black boxes.
  • One-time pricing. We don't trap you in a subscription for documents you'll sign once.
  • Built for indie hackers. Fast, focused, and designed by people who have actually started companies.
  • Instant delivery. Get your customized agreement in minutes, not weeks.

Our values

Transparency first

We show our work. The calculator logic is open and explainable. The agreements clearly state what each clause means.

Founder empathy

We've been there. We know what it's like to bootstrap on ramen and optimism. FairSplit is priced accordingly.

Fairness as a feature

A fair split isn't a nice-to-have. It's the foundation of a healthy, long-term partnership.
