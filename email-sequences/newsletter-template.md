# Weekly Newsletter Template

**Purpose**: Deliver value, build community, share product updates  
**Frequency**: Weekly (Tuesdays, 10am EST)  
**Audience**: All subscribers (waitlist + active users)  
**Sender**: Race <race@changelog.page>  
**Subject Line Pattern**: "[Changelog Weekly] [Intriguing Topic]"

---

## Template Structure

Every newsletter follows this format:

1. **Personal opener** (1-2 sentences)
2. **Main content** (valuable insight/tip)
3. **Quick links** (3 useful resources)
4. **Product update** (what we shipped this week)
5. **Community spotlight** (user showcase)
6. **Closing** (conversation starter)

I spent the weekend analyzing 50+ SaaS changelogs. One pattern stood

---

## The Changelog That 10x'd User Engagement

Meet Jake, founder of a B2B analytics tool.

His changelog was typical: "Added CSV export. Fixed login bug. 
Updated dashboard."

Then he made one change.

Instead of "Added CSV export," he wrote:

"You asked, we built it: Export any report to CSV. Perfect for 
sharing with stakeholders who live in Excel. Pro tip: Combine this 
with scheduled reports for zero-effort weekly updates."

**Result**: That single entry got 340% more clicks than his 
average. Users actually started reading his changelog.

**The lesson**: Features are what you built. Benefits are why 
users care. Lead with benefits.

## Segmentation Strategy

### Segments

1. **Waitlist Only** — Haven't gotten access yet
   - Focus: Building excitement, education
   - Frequency: Weekly

2. **Free Users** — Active but not paying
   - Focus: Success stories, soft Pro mentions
   - Frequency: Weekly

3. **Pro Users** — Paying customers
   - Focus: Advanced tips, exclusive previews
   - Frequency: Weekly + occasional Pro-only updates

4. **Churned** — Cancelled Pro
   - Focus: Win-back, what's new
   - Frequency: Monthly

### Content Adjustments by Segment

| Element | Waitlist | Free | Pro |
|---------|----------|------|-----|
| Product updates | Coming soon | Recently shipped | Beta previews |
| CTAs | Get early access | Upgrade to Pro | Share feedback |
| Content depth | Introductory | Intermediate | Advanced |
| Community spotlights | Inspirational | Peer examples | Pro user features |
