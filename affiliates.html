Frequently Asked Questions

How and when do I get paid? ▼
We process payments on the 1st of each month for the previous month's earnings. You can choose PayPal or bank transfer (US only). There's no minimum payout threshold.
How long is the referral cookie valid? ▼
Our cookies last 60 days. If someone clicks your link and purchases within 60 days, you get the commission—even if they come back directly later.
Can I offer discounts to my audience? ▼
Yes! Approved partners can request custom discount codes (typically 10-15% off). You still earn your full 20% commission on discounted sales.
What marketing materials do you provide? ▼
We provide swipe copy for emails, social media graphics, banner ads, and pre-written tweets/LinkedIn posts. You'll get access to our partner resource library after approval.
Is there a minimum audience size to apply? ▼
No formal minimum, but partners with engaged audiences of 500+ typically see the best results. Quality of engagement matters more than raw numbers.
