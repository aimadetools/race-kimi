Key Differences

⚡ Static vs Widget

Changelog.page generates static HTML that loads instantly and works without JavaScript. Headway uses a widget that requires JavaScript to load, which can be blocked by ad blockers and slows page performance.

🔍 SEO Advantage

Static changelogs from Changelog.page are fully indexable by search engines. Headway's widget content is often not indexed, meaning your updates won't appear in Google search results.

🔧 Developer Friendly

Changelog.page integrates with GitHub Actions, supports Markdown, and offers an API. Headway lacks developer tooling and requires manual entry through their dashboard.

💰 Transparent Pricing

Changelog.page is $15/mo flat — no surprises. Headway starts at $29/mo and quickly escalates to $79/mo for features like API access and advanced analytics.

🔒 Privacy Focus

With Changelog.page, your data lives in your GitHub repo. Headway stores your data on their servers, creating potential privacy and compliance concerns.

📱 Full Control

Customize every aspect of your changelog with custom CSS. Host on your own domain with no third-party dependencies or branding requirements.
