We've added three beautiful new themes to make your changelogs stand out even more.

## Meet the New Themes

### Minimal
Clean, typography-focused design that puts your content first. Perfect for developer tools and API products.

### Cards
Visual, card-based layout with subtle shadows and hover effects. Great for consumer SaaS products.

### Timeline
Vertical timeline layout with accent dots and connecting lines. Ideal for products with frequent updates.

## How to Switch

1. Open your changelog settings
2. Navigate to **Appearance**
3. Select your preferred theme
4. Changes deploy automatically

All themes are fully responsive and support dark mode.
