## What's Coming

Our new analytics dashboard will give you deep insights into how users engage with your changelog:

- **Real-time view tracking** — See which entries are trending right now
- **Traffic source analysis** — Understand where your visitors come from
- **Engagement metrics** — Track time on page, scroll depth, and click-through rates
- **Export capabilities** — Download data for external analysis
