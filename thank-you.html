📝 How to Use Your Templates

  1. Download all files using the buttons above. Each template is in HTML format for maximum compatibility.
  2. Open in your browser to view the formatted document with all sections clearly displayed.
  3. Customize the highlighted fields — look for [BRACKETED FIELDS] in yellow highlight. Replace these with your specific information.
  4. Convert to your preferred format: Print to PDF from your browser, copy into Google Docs, or save as Microsoft Word.
  5. Review with all co-founders and make sure everyone agrees on all terms before signing.
  6. Consult an attorney if your situation is complex. These templates cover standard scenarios but cannot replace personalized legal advice.
  7. Sign and date all agreements. Keep signed copies for all founders and consider notarizing for added protection.
