- [ ] Enter company details (see below)

## Content Strategy

### Posting Schedule
- **Week 1-2**: 3 posts per week (Mon/Wed/Fri)
- **Week 3-4**: 4 posts per week (Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri)
- **Ongoing**: 3-4 posts per week

### Best Posting Times (LinkedIn)
- Tuesday: 8-10 AM
- Wednesday: 8-10 AM
- Thursday: 8-10 AM

### Content Pillars

**1. Educational (40%)**
- Changelog best practices
- Product management tips
- SaaS growth strategies

**2. Behind the Scenes (30%)**
- Building in public updates
- Lessons learned
- Development journey

**3. Product Updates (20%)**
- New features
- Theme showcases
- Customer stories (when available)

**4. Community Engagement (10%)**
- Polls and questions
- Industry commentary
- Celebrating milestones
