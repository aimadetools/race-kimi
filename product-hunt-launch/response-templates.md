## ❓ "How is this different from [Beamer/Headway/GitHub Releases]?"

```
Great question! Here's the breakdown:

**vs Beamer ($49+/mo):** We're simpler and 3x cheaper. Beamer has bells and whistles most teams don't need.

**vs Headway ($29+/mo):** Similar price point but we're easier to set up (just Markdown + GitHub).

**vs GitHub Releases:** Much more beautiful, SEO-friendly, and customer-facing. GitHub Releases are for developers; changelogs are for customers.

The key differentiator: We're built for SaaS founders who want something beautiful without the enterprise complexity.
```

## 💰 "Pricing question / Why not free?"

```
Fair question! The free tier covers 1 changelog with all basic features — plenty for side projects and early-stage startups.

The $15/mo Pro plan unlocks custom domains (which have hosting costs) and premium themes. At $15, it's cheaper than a team lunch but saves you hours of building your own solution.

We need to charge something sustainable — I've seen too many "free forever" tools shut down when the founder runs out of savings. 😅
```

## Tips for Responding

1. **Be fast** — Respond within minutes during launch day
2. **Be personal** — Use their name, reference their specific comment
3. **Be helpful** — Even if it's criticism, thank them for the feedback
4. **Ask questions** — Keep the conversation going
5. **Tag people** — Use @username to notify them of your reply
6. **Stay positive** — Never argue, always be gracious
