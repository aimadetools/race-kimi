# Product Hunt Launch Day Plan

Complete playbook for launch day success.

---

## 📅 Pre-Launch Checklist (1 Week Before)

### 3-4 Days Before
- [ ] Schedule launch date in Product Hunt dashboard
- [ ] Finalize all assets (screenshots, copy, thumbnail)
- [ ] Test all links (website, demo, GitHub)
- [ ] Prepare social media posts for launch day

### 2 Days Before
- [ ] Send "save the date" to supporters
- [ ] Post on Indie Hackers about upcoming launch
- [ ] Schedule Twitter/X announcement

### 1 Day Before
- [ ] Send reminder to supporters (email/DM)
- [ ] Double-check Product Hunter listing
- [ ] Prepare monitoring dashboard (analytics, notifications)
- [ ] Get plenty of sleep! 😴

---

## 🎯 Supporter Outreach Strategy

### Who to Ask
1. **Indie Hackers network** — People who've commented on your posts
2. **Twitter/X followers** — SaaS founders, indie hackers
3. **Reddit communities** — r/SaaS, r/entrepreneur (but be careful about "vote brigading")
4. **Personal network** — Friends who understand Product Hunt
5. **Beta users** — Anyone who's tried the product
6. **Other makers** — Reciprocal upvotes (upvote their launches too)

### Outreach Templates

#### Email/Twitter DM to Friends
```
Hey [name]!

I'm launching Changelog.page on Product Hunt this [Tuesday] and would love your support.

It's a tool that turns Markdown into beautiful changelogs for SaaS products. Think "Beamer but simpler and 3x cheaper."

If you're up for it:
1. Upvote the post (link coming Tuesday)
2. Leave a comment if you have thoughts
3. Share with anyone who might find it useful

No pressure at all — just thought you might be interested!

Thanks 🙏
```

#### Indie Hackers Post

## 📊 Hour-by-Hour Launch Day Schedule

### 12:00 AM PST — Launch Goes Live
- [ ] Post goes live automatically
- [ ] Share link in Indie Hackers "Launch" channel
- [ ] Share link on Twitter/X (midnight post)
- [ ] Send email/DM to "night owl" supporters
- [ ] Start monitoring comments

### 7:00 AM PST — Morning Push (East Coast wake up)
- [ ] Tweet about launch
- [ ] Post on LinkedIn
- [ ] Send morning email to supporters
- [ ] Respond to overnight comments

### 9:00 AM PST — West Coast Wake Up
- [ ] Second Twitter/X post
- [ ] Respond to all new comments
- [ ] Check PH rankings (aim for top 5)

### 12:00 PM PST — Midday Check-in
- [ ] Post update on Indie Hackers
- [ ] Share milestone (e.g., "Top 5! Thank you!")
- [ ] Continue responding to comments

### 3:00 PM PST — Afternoon Push
- [ ] Final social media push
- [ ] Reach out to any influencers who might share
- [ ] Post on relevant subreddits (r/SaaS, r/webdev)

### 6:00 PM PST — Evening Wind Down
- [ ] Thank everyone who supported
- [ ] Post results on Indie Hackers
- [ ] Prepare follow-up plan

### 11:59 PM PST — Launch Day Ends
- [ ] Final ranking determined
- [ ] Celebrate (or commiserate) 🎉

## 🚨 Emergency Protocols

### If Site Crashes
1. Have Vercel dashboard open
2. Check status.vercel.com
3. Post update on PH: "Getting traffic spike, site may be slow"
4. Consider enabling Vercel Pro temporarily for more bandwidth

### If Negative Comments
1. Stay calm and professional
2. Acknowledge their concern
3. Offer solution or explanation
4. Take detailed discussion to DM/email if needed
5. Never argue publicly

### If Technical Issues Reported
1. Acknowledge immediately
2. Create GitHub issue
3. Give ETA for fix
4. Follow up when fixed
5. Thank reporter
